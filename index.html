Note:
  1. PARTICIPATION POINTS:Teams participating in all events will get 100 bonus points.All the points for department events are shared equally among all the 4 classes
  2. JOKER CARD:There will be 2 joker cards i.e. one for class event and one for department event. Each class can put a joker on any class event. If that class wins that event then they will get double the points or else they will have to forfeit 100 points. Similarly each department can put a joker on any department event. If that department wins that event then they will get double the points or else they will have to forfeit 100 points. The joker works only if the team has secured either 1st ,2nd or 3rd place in that particular event. This is applicable for all class and department events as well. Eg. In case of department events if a department had put a joker on a gold event and if that department has won 1st place in that event then all the 4 classes of that department (FE,SE,TE,BE) will receive 400 points each. The joker should be submitted in the online form by 3rd March 5pm and is compulsory.
  3. FACULTY POINTS: If any faculty participates in any department event then that department will automatically get 25 bonus points for that respective event. (Maximum 2 faculty can participate in any department event ) These points can be applied for all department events except for Fashion Show(Rock The Ramp). Faculty cannot participate in Fashion Show(Rock The Ramp). Eg. If a faculty participates for folk dance and another participates for street play in the same department then that department will receive 50 points (25 for folk dance & 25 for street play). Even if a single faculty participates then that department is eligible for 25 points


GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Participants must report at least 15mins before the start of their respective event. Failure to do so might disqualify that team from that event.
  2. Decision of judges is final. Any participant arguing with the judges will lead to his/her disqualification.
  3. Vulgarity of any kind should not be portrayed in any manner, direct or indirect and engaging in vulgar activities will lead to disqualification of the entire team.
  4. Vulgar words are prohibited in the college campus.
  5. Suicidal topics are strictly banned from portraying in any of the events whether on stage or off stage.
  6. Participants have to strictly adhere to the timings mentioned in each of the events.
  7. Submission dates and timings for the photos and videos should be strictly taken into consideration. No photos or videos will be entertained after the appointed time has passed.
  8. Participants have to get their own equipment for all the events mentioned.
  9. There will be 4 departments competing against each other: Civil department, Mechanical department, ETC department, Computer department.
  10. First years are clubbed into their respective department(Civil,Mech,ETC,Comp).
  11. Faculties can participate in all department events except for Solo dance.
  12. Faculties of Humanities Department can join any other department in the department events based on his/her consent.
  13. Faculty members cannot participate in class events
  14. Allocation of joker cards is compulsory for class as well as department events.
  15. Registration process is strictly online on the official PROCYON website ( www.procyon.dbcegoa.ac.in).
  16. Details of all events including the registration form and links to the videos will be hosted on the website.
  17. Each department should allocate a department coordinator (any of the 4 CR’s) to submit the registration form for the department events.
  18. Forms submitted can be edited till 3rd March 2pm. After that editing of the forms will not be allowed.
  19. Each CR will be given a unique ID and password which will enable him/her to access the registration form and to prevent misuse of the form.
  20. Forms should be submitted before 3rd March 2pm. After that the online portal will not accept any forms and the teams will therefore not be able to participate in any event which they haven’t registered for.
  21. All audio and video files should be submitted by 3rd March 2pm. After that entries will not be accepted. The submission should be done to the respective event coordinators only.
  22. Judgement criteria given for the events mentioned in the brochure are tentative and are subject to change based on the decision of the judges.
  23. For fashion show event, each of the departments should compulsorily allocate one faculty member to ensure that the costumes are upto the mark.
  24. Negative marking will take place if the team exceeds its appointed time.
  25. All event timings include stage setup time and performance time. No extra time will be allocated for stage setup.
  26. Venues V1 & V2 will be inside the pandal area.

Food Wizard

AKA : Flameless Cooking
Date : 26th May 2022
Time : 10:30am - 12:30pm
Type : Class
Scoring : Bronze
Venue : V1
Participants : 2 participants per class
Duration : 1Hr 30Mins
Judgement Criteria   : Taste, presentation, hygiene , cooking process.

Instructions :
  1. No alcohol allowed
  2. Power supply will not be provided
  3. Participants cannot make use of fire/flame
  4. Participants can only use all or at least 3 of the following 9 items from the pantry list, while preparing the dish
  5. Pantry :
    1) Eggless mayonnaise, Soya sauce, Mustard sauce, olive oil, ketchup
    2) Bread: poie(bakri), pav, bread, cooked tortilla/roti
    3) Chocolate
    4) Cream, milk, yogurt, coconut cream, condensed milk
    5) Sugar, salt, spices, herbs and gelatin
    6) Cheese, butter, jam, peanut butter
    7) Biscuits
    8) Fresh or cooked vegetables (Onion, tomato, beetroot, lettuce, corn, potato, lemon)
    9) Fruits( Tender coconut, banana, avocado, orange, guava, watermelon)
  6. Participants cannot use any other ingredients other than the one’s mentioned above
  7. A secret ingredient will given by the coordinators at the beginning and the participants have to make that ingredient the highlight of the dish
  8. Marinating, if any , should be done on the spot.
  9. Gloves and head covering is compulsory.
  10. Mobile phones cannot be used while preparing the dish.
  11. One of the participant should be ready to explain the dish prepared to the judges and the list of item used should be mentioned.
  12. Participants are allowed to go fetch ingredients within the same time duration. It is compulsory to present your dish before or at sharp 12:30pm.
